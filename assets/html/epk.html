ELECTRIC TURTLES EPK

Bio

A band formed in the year 100 AD by the name of electrica testudines consisting of 2 members, a close relative of Christ himself, and the devil. After dominating the world and then myseriously dissapearing 1000 years laster, the 2 have reappeared in the year 2021, as Pedro Sanchez, son of Dr. Michael Sanchez, and The Beast From The Middle East, son of Thor. Their current sound is similar to that of the stomping gladiotors, and the wretched depths of rock and roll, which some call hell. Armed with electric guitar and drums, Electric Turtles are unstoppable.

After a 2 years hiatus, ELECTRIC TURTLES are back in action. Their upcoming album, REBIRTH, planned to be released late 2022, is an 8 song masterpiece, recorded at Studio Wong in Berlin. Pedro Sanchez is back on the lead vocals and electric guitar, and Nimrod Lieberman, aka The Beast From the Middle East on drums, and the two create a sonic fusion of rock and roll the world has never heard before. The sound of epic meeting epicness, the two rock harder than rock itself. You can see them live this October, in a city near you.
